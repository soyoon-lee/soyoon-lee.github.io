About My Country, South Korea

South Korea is located in a temperate climate zone.

Due to its location, Korea has four distinct seasons: Spring (March - May), Summer (June - August), Fall (September - November), and Winter (December - February).

Travelers can enjoy different landscapes and activities during each season. In this website, I woud like to introduce representative things you can enjoy in each season.

In Each Season, You Can Enjoy...

  • Spring
    • Cherry Blossom Festival
    • Tulip Festival
    • Canola Festival
  • Summer
    • Beach
    • Water Park
    • Water Leisure
  • Fall
    • Autumn Foliage
  • Winter
    • Snow
    • Skiing, Snowboarding, and Snow Sledding
    • Winter Light Festival
    • Christmas Festival
    • New Year CountDown and Sunrise
